About the club

 This clubs serves the purpose of enhancing and expanding the knowledge of entrepreneurs in the beauty and aesthetics industry. It also brings together people with the same interests and goals and assist each other's hobbies while assessing new trends. This club strives to provide leadership skills, important business skills and opportunities for those with interests in beauty and aesthetics through events and meetings. This club finds young entrepreneurs in the beauty industry and helps them learn and apply business strategies and successful business environments. This club will also provide beauty services to other clubs and associations for specific events that require beauticians. The Laurier community is filled with young entrepreneurs and several types of beauticians, this club will allow for these people to make essential connections that will further enhance their current ideas and strategies or even inspire them to have a business in this type of industry. This club strives combine beauty and business.
